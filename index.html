OPPORTUNITIES AND CHALLENGES FOR RESILIENT FUTURE


organized by
School of Architecture, Planning, and Design,
DIT University, Dehradun
18 - 20 April 2024

Conference Brief

School of Architecture, Planning & Design, SoAPD, DIT University, Dehradun, India brings the 3rd edition of its International Conference in alignment with the recent emergencies faced by global community and sustainable development goals.
In recent times the world has faced unprecedented challenges affecting its inhabitants adversely, natural disasters, climate change, and pandemic has affected public health and food securities causing economic and social disruption that has placed millions of livelihood at great risk. It is the need of the hour to strategize and mitigate these challenges and provide solutions for the consequences of these calamities and to align with SDGâ€™s goals in delivering meaningful progress for people and the world by creating a brighter future for all.

Conference Tracks


The three days blended mode international conference aims to provide a platform for knowledge sharing among artists, academicians, researchers, students, policymakers, and industry professionals from Architecture & Design and Planning & Practices to deliberate upon the following tracks but not limited to:

  • Track 1: Ecological and Inclusive development
  • Track 2: Energy and Sustainability
  • Track 3: Governance & Policies
  • Track 4: Indigenous Design solutions and Technology
  • Track 5: Optimizing existing resources
