Conference Brief

School of Architecture, Planning and Design, DIT University, India brings the 3rd edition of its International Conference in alignment with recent emergencies faced by the global community and the sustainable development goals. In the last few years, apart from the pandemic, catastrophes like war, earthquake, and climate change have had everlasting effects on the global population leading to loss and damage to property and heritage, overcrowding in urban areas, sanitation problems, water shortage, health hazards, the dilapidation of environment quality, increase in crime, cultural crises, and adds to the overall financial crisis. It is the need of the hour to prioritize strategies to mitigate these challenges and provide immediate solutions for the consequences of these calamities faced by the local communities at the urban level. SDG’s 2023 project report also emphasizes delivering meaningful progress for people and the planet by 2030 and creating a brighter future for all. i-Converge 2024, the 3rd edition of the blended mode international conference thus focuses on the “Strategies for a Resilient Future: People, Planet & Progress”.

Conference Tracks


The three days blended mode international conference aims to provide a platform for knowledge sharing among artists, academicians, researchers, students, policymakers, and industry professionals from Architecture & Design and Planning & Practices to deliberate upon the following tracks but not limited:
  • Future Cities
  • Sustainable Urbanism
  • Infrastructure Development
  • Public Policies
  • Regional Practices
  • Disaster Preparation and Response
  • Human Behaviour and Design
  • Cultural Heritage and Development
