General guidelines

  • Author(s) must ensure that the submitted paper is their original work, which has not been previously published.
  • All papers will be reviewed by the International Scientific Committee as per the Advisory Board.
  • By submitting an abstract author(s) agree that if their paper is selected, at least one of the authors will attend the conference.
  • All submitted papers are subject to Double Blind Peer Review process. Authors should not write their names in the final manuscript uploaded contribution.
  • Submissions requiring corrections in response to comments by the technical reviewers must be corrected before the final upload.
  • All submissions must follow the template formats provided and submit the manuscript via online mode only (i.e. Microsoft Conference Management Toolkit).
  • Authors may submit more than one paper as the first author and participate as a co-author on a number of extended abstracts
  • One registration only allows for one paper to be presented and each paper requires a separate registration to be included
  • Select papers may be compiled in a book form with an ISBN number or indexed with Springer/T&F (based upon the quality and approval)
