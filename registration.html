Registration Guidelines


  • You may submit a contribution through submission portal.
  • At least one author of each paper accepted for presentation at the conference must be registered to attend the conference.
  • Each delegate could present a maximum of two papers.
  • However, one can present more than two papers, an additional registration fees per paper to cover related administration costs.
  • There is no restriction on how many papers an individual might be listed on as a co-author.
